HOW TO USE BEARD OIL
1
Empty 4-6 drops of beard oil into the palm of your hand
2
Rub Your hands together and cover them with nourishing
and conditioning oil ready to be applied to facial hair.
3
Run hand through your beard and moustache rubbing into
both the hairs and skin. Start from the neck and work upwards
getting down to the hair roots, then reverse down through
the hairs spreading the oil across the hairs
4
Comb or brush your beard and moustache to evenly distribute
the oil. Now you can face the world armed with a phenomenal
looking beard and tache.
